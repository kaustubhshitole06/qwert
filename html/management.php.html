Management

Management is the process of reaching organizational goals by working with and through people and other resources.

Management is about planning, organizing, leading, and controlling organizational resources to achieve specific goals.

It involves coordinating and optimizing human, financial, and informational resources efficiently.

Effective management ensures organizational success and sustainability.

Management functions include planning, organizing, staffing, directing, and controlling.

Who We Are

N. Gamadia & Co. (NGC) is a renowned Chartered Accountant firm established in 1996, based in Ahmedabad. We have a widespread presence across India, Gujarat & Rajasthan, providing expertise in financial consultancy, audits, SOP design, outsourcing, and tax advisory.

Our team combines deep domain knowledge with advanced technology to deliver tailored solutions, consistently exceeding client expectations for over two decades.
